About Sauti ya Wananchi

Empowering Kenyan citizens to report corruption and service failures through technology and collective action.

Our Mission

To create a transparent and accountable public service system in Kenya by providing citizens with a safe, accessible platform to report corruption, inefficiency, and misconduct in government offices.

Our Vision

A Kenya where public services work for everyone, where corruption is exposed and eliminated, and where every citizen's voice contributes to building a just and equitable society.

How Our Platform Works

We use artificial intelligence to process and organize citizen reports, making them actionable and accessible.

1

Submit Your Report

Citizens can submit reports via text, voice recordings (up to 60 seconds), or images. Reports can be submitted anonymously if preferred.

2

AI Transcription

Voice recordings are automatically transcribed using OpenAI Whisper, supporting both English and Swahili languages.

3

Smart Categorization

Claude AI analyzes each report to extract the category, county, urgency level, involved parties, and generates a clear summary.

4

Public Dashboard

Processed reports appear on our public dashboard with analytics, filters, and visualizations to identify patterns and hotspots.
